INSTRUCTIONS TO THE STUDENTS
  • Strictly abide by the placement policy formed by the Training & Placement cell.
  • Ensure that you only provide correct information while registering for the placements.
  • Register only for the companies which you are interested in.
  • Check this website and your emails regularly.
  • Build your resume professionally and update it accordingly with time.
  • Attend the complete recruitment process including the Pre-placement presentations of the registered companies without fail.
  • Follow the dress code as specified in the placement policy and present yourself professionally.
  • Strictly keep away from malpractices during tests, interviews and other placement events.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
  • How do I get to know which companies are visiting the campus?

    • You can always check this website to know about the companies visiting the campus. In addition to that, Training & Placement team will be sending the emails, regarding the companies, to the student's registered email id. We advise all the students to check the emails regularly to avoid missing any oppurtunity.

  • How do I register for a company?

    • You can see the list of the companies you are eligible for, in the home page of this website. You can click on the "Register" button to register for the interested companies.

  • How do I know if I am eligible for a particular company?

    • We compare the data from your profile against the company's eligibility criteria and display only the companies which you are eligible for. If you can see a company in "Register Now" section in the home page of this website, it means you are eligible to apply for that company.

  • Am I eligible to apply for a company if I am already placed in another company?

    • It depends on the categories of both the companies. We request you to go through the placement policy to get a clear understanding of this topic.
